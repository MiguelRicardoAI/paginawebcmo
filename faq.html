Meals

Where and how do I get my meals?

All meals will be served at the restaurant in Hotel Hacienda Los Laureles. CMO participants will receive a scheduled breakfast, lunch and dinner, for which the menu will be fixed (ie. you will not have a choice of options). If you have any dietary restrictions, please include your request in your online registration or via email at cmo-birs2015@cimat.mx, at least two weeks in advance.

What are the meal costs for guests?

If your guest wishes to join you for meals he/she will be not be able to receive the fixed participant meal, however, may order off the regular hotel menu with a special discount of 20%.

Computers

Are there any computers available for use at the hotels?

Yes. There are four laptops available at Hotel Hacienda Los Laureles for participants to use.

Can I access the Internet from my laptop while at the hotels?

Yes. Each hotel has both ethernet ports as well as wireless internet access.

Can I print from my own laptop while at the workshop?

No. However, for your convenience, there will be a printer available at Hotel Hacienda Los Laureles that may be used with any of the four laptops provided.

Travel

Do I need a visa in order to come to CMO?

For visa Information please visit http://www.inm.gob.mx/index.php/page/paises_visa/en.html. However, we suggest you check with the Embassy of Mexico in the country of origin for any updated information.

CMO can provide visa letters to workshop participants upon request.

Where can I find information for travel funding?

Although CMO provides meals and accommodation to workshop participants free of cost, it unfortunately cannot subsidize travel costs to and from Oaxaca and Hotel Hacienda Los Laureles.

Where do I check in for the workshop?

Check in takes place at Hotel Hacienda Los Laureles which is open 24 hrs. The address is: Calle Hidalgo 21, Colonia Volcanes, 68020 Oaxaca, OAX The phone number is: +01 951 501 5300.

What are my transportation options for getting to CMO?

Book your flight to the Xoxocotlán International Airport (OAX), in Oaxaca. For detailed information on transportation to your hotel, please click here.

Town of Oaxaca Information What are the climate conditions in Oaxaca ? Over the course of a year, the temperature in Oaxaca typically varies from 9°C to 31°C and is rarely below 6°C or above 34°C. Good sources for this information are https://weatherspark.com/averages/32580/Oaxaca-Mexico and http://espanol.weather.com/weather/today/Oaxaca+OA+M%C3%A9xico+MXOA0069:1:MX. What are some good places to visit in Oaxaca and surroundings? There are several amazing places to visit in Oaxaca. You can find information at http://oaxacatours.mx and request information at info @oaxacaexperts.com. What are the electrical systems and plug types used in Oaxaca ? Mexico uses 127 V, 60 Hz electrical outlets, the same as those found in the U.S. and Canada. Where can I exchange currencies? It is highly advised to exchange currencies in your country of origin; your personal bank will provide you with the most accurate exchange rate, often without charging a commission fee. Airport currency exchanges are not advised, as they can charge exorbitant commission fees. There are several currency exchanges in downtown Oaxaca, and a bus from the hotels to downtown that takes about 15 minutes. Please note that many businesses in Oaxaca may not accept any currency other than cash; therefore, it is highly advisable that you obtain some local currency to use. What is the etiquette around tipping? Please be advised that gratuities for hotel staff have not been included as part of your participation in the CMO workshop. Hotel staff earn a low wage and are very grateful for your recognition of their hard work. A suggestion of at least /$15 USD for hotel staff is encouraged at the end of your stay. For information on tipping guidelines in and around Oaxaca city, please visit Trip Advisor.
