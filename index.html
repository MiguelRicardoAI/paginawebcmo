Casa Matemática Oaxaca

The new BIRS-affiliated mathematics research centre, Casa Matemática Oaxaca (CMO), will be located in the city of Oaxaca, Mexico. Oaxaca is a UNESCO designated World Heritage site. Construction of new facilities will be underway shortly. In the mean time, CMO is operating out of the Hotel Hacienda Los Laureles, Hotel Angel Inn and Las Cúpulas, in the city of Oaxaca.

Accommodation

Accommodation is at the Hotel Hacienda Los Laureles, Hotel Angel Inn and Las Cúpulas, in Oaxaca city. Accommodation and meals are provided free of charge during the workshop dates (Sunday night to mid-day Friday), for invited participants only. If you bring guests, they will have to pay for their own meals. As well, there will be an additional room charge per night for guests: Hotel Hacienda Los Laureles - 714 Mexican pesos (including taxes); Hotel Angel Inn - 238 Mexican pesos (including taxes); Hotel Las Cupulas - 200 Mexican pesos (including taxes).

If you wish to arrive before the workshop begins or stay after the workshop ends, you must arrange and pay for your own accommodation. The hotels will offer a 30% discount to CMO participants, however, due to other workshops, there may not be availability. Prices range from $167 to $287 USD per night. Other accommodation may be found using Trip Advisor.

Questions?

For answers to the most common participant inquiries, please visit our FAQ page. Any other queries about the logistics on site in Oaxaca should be directed to Dr. Silvia Millán López .

Please note that while BIRS provides logistical support for the activities at CMO, is not responsible for any aspect of its local operations. Participants assume responsibility for their own travel and well being as is customary for conferences of this nature.

Reply to an Invitation

To accept or decline an invitation to a CMO workshop (in Mexico), please visit https://www.birs.ca/cmo-rsvp.

Potential Participants

If you are interested in attending a CMO workshop, please refer to the guidelines below.

For Faculty: Please email the BIRS Program Coordinator, who will then submit your request to the organizers of the workshop you are interested in attending.

For Students and Postdocs: Please email the BIRS Program Coordinator with a brief letter of recommendation from your supervisor. We will then submit your request to the organizers of the workshop you are interested in attending.
